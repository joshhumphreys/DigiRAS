Relief at Source interim claim

Use this online form to:

  • make an interim claim for tax relief at source on pension contributions
  • tell us about any excess relief that you’ve claimed
Start now

Before you start

If you're using your Managing Pension Schemes account to make an interim claim, this online form replaces the APSS 105 form. You can still make a claim by post or email if you want.

You should submit this form no later than the last working day of the month of the claim so that HMRC can pay you on the 21st day of the following month (or the next working day). For example, you must send a claim to 5 June by the last working day of June. HMRC will then send payment on 21 July (or the next working day).

For more information, you can read the full Relief at Source guidance page and page PTM044220 of the Pensions Tax Manual.
