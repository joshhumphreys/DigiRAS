You need to make a claim by email or post

If you are reporting contributions as a result of the transfer of assets in lieu of a cash contribution debt, you cannot use this online service. You need to download and fill in an APSS 105 form to make your interim claim.

Once you've done this, email the file to example@hmrc.co.uk. An authorised signatory will need to send the email. Alternatively, you can post the form to: Pension Schemes Services, HM Revenue and Customs, BX9 1GH.

You can find more information on the full Relief at Source guidance page and page PTM042100 of the Pensions Tax Manual.

You can return to your relief at source dashboard.
