You need to download and fill in an excess schedule

If you are reporting excess as part of your interim claim, you need to download and fill in an excess relief schedule template.

Once you've done this, email the file to example@hmrc.co.uk. You can then continue with your interim claim.

Alternatively, you can post the excess schedule to: Pension Schemes Services, HM Revenue and Customs, BX9 1GH.

You can find more information on the excess relief at source guidance page.
